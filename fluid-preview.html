Project
The Fluid 1.5㎡
2025
"The Fluid 1.5㎡" examines the right to rest for urban support labour. It reveals that break spaces for cleaners, guards and others are not fixed but fluid: temporary corners averaging just 1.5㎡, tucked under stairs or in storage rooms. Through eight spatial collages and digital narratives, the project documents material clutter, cramped postures and fragmented time in these zones.
